What is Alamy?

Alamy is an AI-powered dream journal website that allows users to record and analyze their dreams. The website is designed to provide a platform for users to log their dreams, receive insights and interpretations, and track patterns over time. Although we are currently unable to incorporate AI due to limitations in knowledge and resources, we will soon simulate AI functionality using JavaScript libraries.

FAQs


How does it work?

Dream Logging:

Users can record details of their dreams, including title, date, description, and any associated emotions or themes.

Dream Analysis:

The website utilizes JavaScript libraries to analyze the dream descriptions and extract keywords, emotions, and themes. This analysis is then used to provide users with insights and interpretations of their dreams.

Dream Visualization:

Users can view their dream data through charts and graphs, allowing them to track patterns and trends over time.

Dream Sharing:

Users have the option to share their dreams with others through social media platforms.

How Accurate is the AI Interpretation of Dreams?

This website plans to use AI to recognize patterns and generate appropriate responses. It does not give medical or spiritual advice.

Can I Share My Dreams on Social Media?

Features will soon be available to comment and share your dreams and interpretations on the website as well as on social media platforms.
